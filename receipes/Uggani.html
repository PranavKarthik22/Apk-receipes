Uggani receipe

It is a simple dish for breakfast with puffed rice. It is healthy and tasty as well.

Ingredients

  • Puffed rice
  • Sunflower oil
  • Mustard seeds
  • Jeera
  • Curry leaves

    • ---Vegitables Sliced---

  • Green Chillies
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Gun Powder

Steps

  1. Switch on the stove and place a bowl.
  2. Add Sunflower oil - 2tsps.
  3. After 2 mins add Mustard seeds, jeera, curry leaves, green chillies, a pinch of turmeric and cook for 5-10 mins.
  4. Add onions and tomato cook for 10-15mins
  5. Meanwhile, soak the puffed rice ,squeeze and keep it aside.
  6. mix this puffed rice with gun powder.
  7. Add the puffed rice to the cooking bowl and mix it well
  8. Sprinkle some coriander leaves and Done, Uggani is ready!!!
