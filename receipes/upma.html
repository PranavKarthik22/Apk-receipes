Upma receipe

It is a simple dish for breakfast with rava.

Ingredients

  • Banci rava
  • Sunflower oil
  • Mustard seeds
  • Jeera
  • Curry leaves

    • ---Vegitables Sliced---

  • Green Chillies
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • Potato
  • Carrot
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Switch on the stove and place a bowl.
  2. Add Sunflower oil - 2tsps.
  3. After 2 mins add Mustard seeds, jeera, curry leaves, green chillies and cook for 5-10 mins.
  4. Add onions and cook until golden brown
  5. Now, add all the remaining vegitables and cook
  6. Once cooked enough add water based on the measurement of rava i.e for 1 glass of rice 2 1/2 cups of water and bring it to bowl.
  7. Add rava to it and salt. Stir slowly until all the water is absorbed.
  8. Sprinkle some coriander leaves and Done, Upma is ready!!!
